Sean Lockwood is an artist currently working as an instructional technician supporting photography and interdisciplinary visual art students at the University of Washington. He received a BFA from the Maryland Institute College of Art in 2016 and an MFA from the University of Washington in 2019. His studio practice is concerned with production and decision making under conditions of partial information and inconsistent affordance.

Myself and Nick Strobelt currently run Paid, a garage art space in the North Beacon Hill neighborhood of Seattle. Visit paid.exchange
